AI Operational Systems Case Study (2023)

Production AI-native workflow system built inside a small agency environment. Event-driven onboarding, AI content generation, supervisory optimization loops, and CRM orchestration.

Overview

In mid-2023, a small digital marketing agency lost a sales director and needed a way to support a young and relatively inexperienced sales staff with better operational context and workflow consistency.

The original proposal was modest: extend the agency’s existing CRM with a wiki-like internal knowledge layer containing lead history, objections, prior interactions, and organizational context.

The initial project brief was drafted in approximately 90 minutes during a strategy discussion with ownership.

During drafting, scope expanded rapidly into:

  • lead scoring
  • sentiment analysis
  • workflow automation
  • AI-assisted content generation
  • automated onboarding
  • recursive optimization systems
  • operational augmentation for sales + marketing

The system evolved from CRM extension → operational orchestration layer.

System Architecture

  • ExpressionEngine module as orchestration layer
  • API-based AI workflows
  • event-driven provisioning system
  • cron-based supervisory cycle
  • human-in-the-loop validation
  • modular provider abstraction for model swap flexibility

Event-Driven Onboarding

Payment event triggered full system provisioning:

  • CRM record creation
  • Slack channel provisioning
  • workflow initialization
  • onboarding form generation

After onboarding submission:

  • website scraping (Python)
  • structured training/context creation
  • AI model adaptation via OpenAI API
  • initial content generation pipeline

Target behavior: signup at night → production-ready outputs by next morning.

AI Content System

  • blogs, SEO, social content generation
  • client-specific contextual modeling
  • historical ingestion of brand data
  • ~23-prompt orchestration chain

Supervisory Optimization Loop

  • aggregated engagement + analytics
  • weekly optimization cycle (cron)
  • recommendations per client system
  • human edits as feedback signals
  • accept/reject structured responses

This created a recursive improvement loop between generation and human correction.

Sales System

  • lead enrichment
  • lead scoring
  • sentiment overlays
  • automated follow-ups
  • CRM augmentation layer

Operational Outcomes

  • ~60 days → ~3 weeks onboarding
  • <2 hour response time across ~90 clients
  • ~4x revenue growth in ~70 days
  • reduced operational load without headcount growth
