Origin & System Formation

CRM Wiki → AI Operational System

The system began in 2023 during a transition period inside a small agency that had just lost its sales director. The initial intent was not automation but augmentation: a CRM extension that would act as a lightweight wiki layer for junior sales staff.

During a ~90 minute drafting session with leadership, the idea expanded rapidly from “context tooling” into a full operational redesign.

As the system brief evolved, the architecture shifted toward:

  • event-driven CRM workflows instead of static records
  • automated onboarding triggered by payment events
  • AI-assisted content generation tied to client state
  • recurring optimization loops across accounts

At this stage, the system stopped being a tool and became an operational layer.

Architecture

Modular AI Workflow System

  • ExpressionEngine module as orchestration layer
  • API-driven AI integration (OpenAI endpoints)
  • event-driven provisioning system
  • cron-based master scheduling loop
  • modular provider abstraction for model swapping

The system was intentionally modular to anticipate rapid evolution of underlying AI models.

Onboarding System

Event-Driven Client Provisioning

  • payment event triggers full system setup
  • Slack + CRM + workspace provisioning
  • automated onboarding form generation
  • site scraping + historical data capture
  • brand modeling via AI ingestion

A client could sign up at night and receive structured outputs the next business cycle.

Content System

AI Content + Brand Modeling Layer

  • blogs, social, SEO content generation
  • client-specific tone modeling
  • historical content ingestion (up to 2 years)
  • ~23 prompt orchestration chain

Generated outputs were routed through human review before publishing.

Feedback System

Master → Client Recursive Loop

  • engagement + analytics ingestion
  • master model evaluation cycle
  • weekly cron-based optimization
  • human edits as structured feedback signals
  • accept/reject training loop

This created a bidirectional learning system between production outputs and supervisory evaluation.

Outcomes

Operational Impact

  • ~4x revenue growth in ~70 days
